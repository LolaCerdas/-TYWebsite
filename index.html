An emerging
media playground.

NYU Tandon @ The Yard leverages the Emerging Media expertise of NYU Tandon School of Engineering with industry and media partners across the country to create a new media hub and must-visit facility within the Brooklyn Navy Yard. Our goals are to advance integrative research in AR/VR/XR, virtual production, and other topics relevant to experiential computing; provide our industry partners with first-hand contact with both NYU faculty, students, and media organizations in New York City.
